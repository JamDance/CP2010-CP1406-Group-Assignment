Advanced piano students! A Masterclass opportunity with internationally acclaimed pianist Roman Rudnytsky.

Presented by Townsville City Council, Roman will be in Townsville for a performance on Thursday June 4th at 8pm and will hold a free masterclass on Wednesday 3rd June at 6:30pm. Both of these events will be held in C2, at the rear of the Civic Theatre. The performance is ticketed, and can be Booked Online

Roman Rudnytsky, an American concert pianist of Ukrainian background, has been actively performing around the world for many years. He is a graduate of the Juilliard School in New York, where he worked with famed pianist pedagogue Rosina Lhevinne, and later studied in Italy, directed by Beethoven expert Wilhelm Kempff and at the Mozarteum in Salzburg, Austria. Roman’s great passion is education and he is currently “Professor Emeritus” of the Dana School of Music of Youngstown State University in Youngstown, Ohio, USA, a position he has held for 39 years. Roman also conducts master classes and workshops for local piano students and teachers during the course of his travels. He has played concerts and conducted master classes in more than 100 countries. Since 1979 he has visited Australia every two years, playing more concerts here than in any other country – approximately 550 at last count!
The more advanced the student, the better this masterclass will work!

Volunteers required!

To promote live performances, we must keep production costs to a minimum. The Townsville City Council assists us by allowing many tasks to be done by volunteers. Most volunteer activities do not require any special skills e.g. setting out or stacking chairs.

Ushers play a special role, taking tickets at the door and ensuring the comfort and safety of all patrons. A brief, free training session is compulsory for this role, to learn theatre policy, emergency evacuation procedures, etc. Ushers are then registered with Civic Theatre management and may work at many theatre performances throughout the year. Please call 4724 2086 if you are interested in taking part in these rewarding activities.

Our Live House Music service

Performance opportunities for music students.

The Music Centre have a series of Sunday afternoon concerts at C2 and Friends of the Theatre have regular variety concerts at Pimlico PAC, also on Sunday afternoons. Because these venues have no foyer space, the theatre has to be open about an hour before the concert, so that audience members don't have to stand outside in the sun, wind, and rain... Instead of playing bland recorded music before the show, we prefer to have student musicians playing on stage when the doors open.

For the musicians, this is effectively a practice session with a live audience; they don't have to "perform", they can stop and start as they please. They need to be of reasonably high standard, but they don't have to be polished performers. About 15 minutes before the concert start time, our compere introduces them, acknowledges the contribution of their school, and the curtain closes. The students are then welcome to stay for the concert. The students are considered volunteers, but we can help pay for expenses incurred. Small instrumental groups are preferred. The offer is open to all schools, so we need to schedule in advance. FOTT have 6 concerts per year, the Music Centre about 10. If you would like to take part, please let us know so we can send you a list of concert dates as soon as they are confirmed. Call us on 4724 2086 or Email Us

Here's How It Works

The Bulletin Board is a free service, intended not only for selling or advertising, but also for any items of news or general interest. Photos may be included.

Any person can contribute an item, but each item will be rejected or accepted by its relevance to the "musical community," that is, musicians, teachers, students, etc. The contributor is responsible for the integrity of the information they provide. Music Centre will not be involved in any way in the sale of goods advertised on this page. Items will be published for an appropriate time; say 2 weeks for a photo or news item, up to 2 months for items on sale.

For more information, Email Us or mail to Townsville Community Music Centre, PO Box 1006, Townsville Q. 4810
